Why Arena Exists

Built in the classroom.
Scaled for national reform.

Arena Hub began as a single school's answer to a broken evidence gap. It is now moving to a national pilot — because that gap is everywhere.

The Origin

Designed by a practitioner to run a foundation-led AP site — a "Perfect School" stalled by legacy tech fear and Big Tech lock-in. Arena broke that stranglehold and is now entering its national pilot phase.

The Architecture

Sovereign Hybrid design. All student data remains inside your school's own Google Workspace tenant. No central databases. No third-party data exposure. Full ICO & UK GDPR Article 28 compliance by design.

The Outcome

The Equalizer Package. We convert vocational agency and social progress into forensic evidence — professional credentials that rival GCSE folders and are recognised at LA and national level.
