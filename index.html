The challenge

AP evidence is complex.
Your tools shouldn't be.

Practitioners in Alternative Provision carry the weight of complex need, incomplete histories, and high-stakes decisions — often without the systems to back them up.

Evidence scattered across systems

Email threads, printed observations, WhatsApp notes. When Ofsted knocks, the evidence isn't ready.

No audit trail

Without a timestamped, tamper-evident record, decisions made in good faith can't be defended.

GDPR risk on every submission

Third-party platforms holding student PII is a compliance liability most AP settings aren't aware of.

Tools built for mainstream

MIS systems assume a register, year groups, and a timetable. AP doesn't work like that.
